Our Story

A legacy of hospitality on the shores of Uzungöl

Tradition Meets Excellence

Since 2009, Ada Restaurant has been a beacon of culinary excellence in Uzungöl, where the pristine waters meet the majestic mountains of the Black Sea region.

Our journey began with a simple vision: to create a dining destination that honors the rich culinary heritage of the Black Sea while embracing contemporary elegance and international standards of service.

Today, we proudly serve guests from around the world, offering them not just a meal, but an unforgettable experience that captures the essence of Turkish hospitality.

2009 - The Beginning

Ada Restaurant opens its doors, introducing a new standard of lakeside dining to Uzungöl

2020 - Recognition

Awarded "Best Regional Cuisine" by Turkish Culinary Federation

2022 - Expansion

Launch of our exclusive private dining rooms and event spaces

2024 - Innovation

Introduction of our seasonal tasting menu featuring sustainable, locally-sourced ingredients
